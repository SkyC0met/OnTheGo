Welcome to Teensy Town!

Teensy Town houses rabbits and guinea pigs

Come by to play, pet and feed these adorable furry friends

Gizmo
The Cuddler

Gizmo

Gizmo absolutely loves being around humans! Whenever visitors come to the petting zoo, he eagerly approaches them with his adorable wheeks and squeaks, seeking attention and cuddles. He enjoys gentle strokes and often sits contentedly on people's laps, basking in all the affection he receives.
Pippin
The Gourmet Connoisseur

Pippin

Pippin adores food! Whether it's fresh veggies like crisp lettuce and juicy carrots or tasty hay and crunchy pellets, Pippin can't resist indulging in the scrumptious delights. It's a delightful sight to see Pippin munching away happily, and his joyous "wheek-wheeks" are his way of expressing gratitude for the delicious treats!
Midnight
The Shy One

Midnight

Midnight is more reserved and hesitant than her siblings, especially around new people or in unfamiliar environments. When approached, Midnight may retreat to the safety of a cozy hiding spot or burrow into some soft bedding. However, once Midnight becomes familiar with her human friends, she starts to warm up and display her curious and affectionate nature.
Cottontail
The Silly One

Cottontail

Cottontail is the epitome of silliness! This adorable little fluffball has a penchant for the comical and quirky. Whether it's hopping around in playful zig-zags, doing bunny binkies with uncontainable joy, or engaging in hilarious antics during playtime, Cottontail never fails to bring a smile to everyone's face.
Thumper
The Cool One

Thumper

Thumper is the epitome of coolness! This suave and charismatic bunny has a laid-back demeanor that sets them apart from the rest. Thumper effortlessly exudes confidence with every hop and bounce, and their cool and collected attitude makes them the center of attention wherever they go.

Welcome to Medows!

Medows houses lambs and goats

Come by to play, pet and feed these adorable furry friends

Daisy
The Excited One

Daisy

Daisy is the epitome of excitable energy. From the moment she was born, her sprightly and lively nature was apparent. Her soft, white fleece bounces with every skip and jump she takes, adding to her adorable charm. Whenever she spots something new or interesting, her eyes widen with excitement, and her little tail wags with delight.
Willow
The Agile One

Willow

Willow is incredibly agile. With nimble hooves and a keen sense of balance, he moves gracefully across rocky terrains and climbs steep slopes with ease. Whether it's navigating through a maze of fallen logs or leaping from one boulder to another, Willow seems to have an inherent ability to handle challenging landscapes with finesse.
Jasper
The Playful One

Jasper

Jasper is renowned for being the most playful and mischievous member of the entire goat herd. From dawn till dusk, he can be found engaging in all sorts of entertaining antics that keep the other goats amused and on their hooves. His playful charm is a constant reminder of the joy that can be found in the simplest of moments.

Welcome to Hay Stacks!

Hay Stacks houses ponies

Moonbeam
The Sweet One

Moonbeam

Moonbeam is exceptionally sweet and gentle. She has a calming aura that puts everyone at ease, making her a favorite among children and adults alike. Her soft, silver mane sparkles like the night sky, and her eyes radiate warmth and kindness. Moonbeam loves to nuzzle and share affection with anyone who comes her way, and her playful nature often brings smiles to the faces of those around her.
Thunderstrike
The Curious One

Thunderstrike

Thunderstrikeis known for his insatiable curiosity. From a young age, he displayed an eagerness to explore and discover the world around him. Whenever there's a new object, sound, or scent, Thunderstrike can't help but investigate with wide eyes and a twitching nose. This curiosity often leads him on adventures beyond the confines of their pasture, with their hooves carrying him to far-off lands as they seek new experiences.
